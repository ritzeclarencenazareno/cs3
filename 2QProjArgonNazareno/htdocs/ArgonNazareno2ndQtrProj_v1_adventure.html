Lost in Ilocos

Me and my family like to travel a lot when there si time, in fact, we go out on a vacation every summer, we've been through a lot of fun experiences, but one of the most memorable ones is the time we went to ilocos. We were so excited to get there. We had hoped to get there fast and take pictures and have fun. However, that was not the case for us. On the way to Ilocos, we faced a lot of traffic that drastically hindered our speed.As if that wasn't bad enough, we got lost in our way and had to find our way back in the heavy traffic. After almost a day of driving, we finally made it to our destination. The view was beautiful, the food was delicous, and the environment was refreshing. On our first day there, we went swimming in the blue ocean, played games and had fun all day.

We used the rest of our time in Ilocos to go sightseeing and going to famous tourist spots. Overall, I loved the experience and would recommend anyone to visit Ilocos.
