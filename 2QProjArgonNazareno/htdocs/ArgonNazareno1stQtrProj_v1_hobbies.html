My Hobbies

I have a lot of hobbies, but I will only include here what I like best. One of my notable hobbies is playing badminton with my cousins. Getting a break from all the gadgets everyday while having fun is a good way to refresh for another week. It is also a good way to exercise. Another one of my hobbies is talking to my friends, I find it really fun to talk to my close friends even in this pandemic. It is also a very good way to unwind from all the stress i experience. One more of my hobbies is playing games especially if it's online with my friends or cousins. Genshin Impact, Call of Duty(CoD), and Cookie Run Kingdom(CRK) are the games I play. I play Genshin Impact and CRK because I like its lore, while I play CoD because I like its pvp gameplay and CoD is a pvp game I can play with my friends.
